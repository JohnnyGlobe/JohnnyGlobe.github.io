    10 years of expirience in Mandarin teaching

    Our courses are designed for the serious learners, our students go from zero to work level profeciency in 16 weeks.

    Mandarin Pro has over 10 years of experiences teaching Mandarin to businessmen, entrepreneurs, ambassadors, political figures, legal workers, and other professionals and/or hobbyists who seek to understand and potentially explore professional endeavors in China someday. We strive to help you with not only language learning, but also connecting you to the resources you need and providing everything we can to help you succeed.

    Mandarin Pro employs the best teachers in the industry. Our proprietary courses are designed to help students reach everyday conversation level in 16 weeks. Students who passed the HSK Level 4 (6 levels in total) can request domain specific language courses from our teachers. For example, students who work in the filming industry can request customized lessons on vocabularies and specific jargons in cinematography. Our teachers will then design a course for just that！

We pay lots to our teachers to they can deliver the best experience to our students.
