10 years of experience in Mandarin teaching

Our courses are designed for the serious learners. Our students get from zero to work level proficiency in 20 weeks.

Mandarin Pro has over 10 years of experiences teaching Mandarin to businessmen, entrepreneurs, ambassadors, political figures, legal workers, and other professionals and/or hobbyists who seek to understand and potentially explore professional endeavors in China someday. We strive to help you with not only learning the language, but also connecting you to the resources you need and providing everything we can to help you succeed.

Mandarin Pro employs the best teachers in the industry. Our proprietary courses are designed to help students reach everyday conversation and working level in 20 weeks. Students who passes the HSK (standardized test for Mandarin) Level 4 will qualify for domain-specific language courses. For example, students who work in the filming industry can request customized lessons on jargons and nomenclature in cinematography. If your industry is niche, our teachers will design a course just for you！
