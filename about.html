What is MandarinPro?


Mandarin is the most widely spoken language in the world. In the past two decades, China has become the quintessential landmark of opportunities. Unfortunately, not everyone can ride this economic rocket ship. In order to participate, you need to speak and read Mandarin, well.

So, what is the problem? Mandarin is hard. But too many people don’t realize that. The go-to method today for most people to learn Mandarin is still self-teaching language apps, watching youtube videos, try to learn from a friend and even trusting AI-powered practice questions. Its no wonder we seldom see an expat speaking fluent Mandarin. These methods simply don't work. If you want to speak and read at the level you desire, you need a personalized interactive lesson plan and a well-versed private tutor.

Mandarin Pro was founded on the principle of quality teaching. We will do whatever it takes to make sure our students learn the language as quickly as possible. We have been in the business of helping foreign professionals succeed in China for a decade. Our students have high standards for us to meet. So, we spent years designing and developing our own curriculum. Our teaching methods have been tried and trued with hundreds of students in the past, you can check out some feedbacks here.

If you are serious about learning Mandarin, then try Mandarin Pro. Here you will join a big family of serious Mandarin learners, well connected founders, and an elite alumni network of professionals pursuing or planning on pursuing opportunities in China.

Make friends, learn Mandarin, and have fun at MandarinPro :)

Curriculum Design



Professor Lv Bi Song, founder of Chinese National Language Office

Our proprietary language course is developed based on the work of respected Professor Lv Bi Song (吕必松), Dean of Chinese as a Foreign Language of Beijing Language and Culture University, and leader of Chinese National Language Office (国家汉办). Mr Lv has had many academic achievements through out his life in the field of Chinese as a Foreign Language, including founding the department of Chinese as a foreign language in the Beijing Language and Culture University, training the initial batch of teachers and operating the department. He also created the masters program of Chinese as a Foreign Language in the university and created the syllabus for the program. Mr Lv also founded a research lab in the field of Chinese as a Foreign Language.

10 years ago we tried almost all curriculum on the market in search of an efficient method to provide to our students. However, the materials we tried were always either too theoretical or too shallow for students who are looking to get up to speed efficiently. So we began to develop our own curriculum with the best mix of theory and practicality. We developed our courses alongside a former student of Mr Lv Bi Song and married his teaching philosophies with our students' needs. A decade later, we are confident to say that our current curriculum is second to none when it comes to effective learning for serious students. We are able to take a total beginner in Mandarin to HSK Level 4 (casual everyday language level) in less than 20 weeks.
