We connect you to the right people

Knowing Mandarin is only half the battle... knowing people is the other half

Over the years, after working with hundreds of foreign professionals and helping them succeed in China, Mandarin Pro has harvested a group of high-caliber students and friends. By joining Mandarin Pro, you are also joining a global network of diverse foreign leaders who are invested in China. It is always a pleasure to make friends with people of similar experiences and aspirations. We host monthly events and find plenty of opportunities for our students to mix and mingle outside of the classroom.
